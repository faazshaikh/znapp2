Most drivers complete onboarding and get approved within 24–48 hours. The steps are straightforward and our team is here to help if anything gets stuck.

You'll need a few basics — we walk you through every step during onboarding.

  • Driver's License — valid, not expired
  • Vehicle Registration — current, in your name
  • Insurance — proof of current auto insurance
  • Background Check — we help you complete this
  • Vehicle Inspection — basic safety check (we can arrange this)
  • Smartphone — iPhone iOS 14+ or Android 8.0+

We accept most personal vehicles in good working condition. Specific year and model requirements vary by city contract. Generally:

  • 4-door vehicles preferred for passenger routes
  • Trucks, vans, and SUVs accepted for certain contracts
  • Must pass a basic safety inspection
  • Must be registered and insured in your name

ZNAPP! runs a standard background check covering driving history and criminal record. We partner with a trusted third-party provider to make this easy and fast — usually takes less than 24 hours.

You earn for every trip you complete. You always see your payout before accepting — no surprises.

  • Passenger Rides — local trips and commuter routes
  • Roadway Asset Collection — capture images while driving your normal routes
  • Municipal Contracts — special city-approved transportation gigs
  • Peak Bonuses — extra pay during high-demand hours

Earnings are deposited directly to your bank account on a weekly schedule. You can track every dollar in real time from the driver dashboard — no hidden fees, no vague breakdowns.

Active drivers on ZNAPP! average $320+ per week. Earnings vary based on city, contract type, hours driven, and whether you pick up bonuses and asset-collection routes alongside rides.

Driver safety is a top priority. ZNAPP! is built in partnership with cities and municipalities — not just an app — so safety infrastructure is baked in.

  • All riders are verified before any trip is accepted
  • Municipal route contracts come with city oversight
  • In-app emergency contact and real-time location sharing

When you drive your regular routes, the ZNAPP! app can automatically capture images of road conditions, signage, and infrastructure. This data is shared with city partners to improve road safety.
