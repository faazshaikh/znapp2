Most drivers complete onboarding and receive approval within 24–48 hours. Our team is available to help if anything gets stuck.

A few basics are required — we walk you through every step during onboarding so nothing gets missed.

  • Driver's License — valid and not expired
  • Vehicle Registration — current, in your name
  • Insurance — proof of current auto insurance
  • Background Check — we help you complete this
  • Smartphone — iPhone iOS 14+ or Android 8.0+

We accept most personal vehicles in good condition. Specific requirements vary by city contract — generally:

  • 4-door vehicles preferred for passenger routes
  • Trucks, vans, and SUVs accepted for certain contracts
  • Must pass a basic safety inspection
  • Must be registered and insured in your name

A standard check covering driving history and criminal record through a trusted third-party provider. Most results come back within 24 hours.

You earn for every completed trip — and you always see your payout before accepting it. No surprises.

  • Passenger Rides — local trips and commuter routes
  • Roadway Asset Collection — earn extra capturing road images on your normal routes
  • Municipal Contracts — special city-approved transportation gigs
  • Peak Bonuses — extra pay during high-demand hours

Earnings are deposited directly to your bank account on a weekly schedule. Track every dollar in real time from the driver dashboard — no hidden fees, no vague breakdowns.

Active drivers on ZNAPP! average $320+ per week. Earnings vary by city, contract type, hours driven, and bonuses or asset-collection routes on top of rides.
