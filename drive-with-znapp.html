What you need

Requirements

We keep the bar straightforward. If you can legally drive, you're most of the way there.

Documents Required

  • Valid driver's license (not expired)
  • Current vehicle registration in your name
  • Proof of active auto insurance
  • Government-issued photo ID

Vehicle Required

  • 4-door vehicle, 2010 or newer
  • Pass a basic safety inspection
  • Clean interior and exterior
  • Working seatbelts in all seats

Background check Required

  • Standard criminal background check
  • Clean driving record (last 3 years)
  • No major traffic violations
  • We'll help you complete the process

Device Needed

  • iPhone (iOS 14+) or Android (8.0+)
  • Active data plan for in-trip navigation
  • Phone mount recommended (not required)
  • Charging cable for long shifts
